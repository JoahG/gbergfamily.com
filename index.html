Howdy

We are a family with seven (wonderful) children that tries to stay busy with things that matter - for eternity. We seek to encourage one another along our journey this side of heaven. We want to live our lives in thankful honor to Jesus Christ, because of the gift of eternal life we have in Him. If we make a mistake (or several), pray for us and remind us of what the Word instructs us to do... and be gracious. Perhaps we will encourage you in your pursuit of real life, too.
